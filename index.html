A Silent Voice


This film covers elements of coming of age and psychological drama. Dealing with themes such as bullying, forgiveness, and mental health. It has the story of a former bully that turned into a social outcast, who decides to reconnect and befriend the deaf girl he had bullied years prior.

The Main Characters

  • Shōko Nishimiya
  • Shoya Ishida
